UmpCall Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 27, 2026

No ads No analytics SDKs Offline-first Optional cloud sync

Privacy at a glance

UmpCall is offline-first. Toss, counter, and solo scoring stay on your device unless you choose club sync, live follow, recovery, or handover.

Terms Support

Information stored on your device

Unless you choose to sync or publish a live match, UmpCall stores cricket data locally on your phone or tablet.

Match dataCounter state, toss history, teams, rosters, match history, scorecards, and ball-by-ball scoring.
PreferencesUmpire nickname, local club label, theme, haptics, voice, rules, and coin selection.
Custom coin imagesCopied from your photo library to app storage only. We do not upload custom coin images.

Optional cloud features

When you use club, live match, recovery, or handover features, UmpCall sends only the data needed for those features over HTTPS to backend infrastructure.

  • Anonymous session identifier for membership and recovery.
  • Club names, team names, player names, rosters, scores, match state, and live score snapshots.
  • Join, live match, handover, and recovery codes used for access control.

You control what goes online by choosing club, sync, live match, or handover features. Solo scoring, toss, and counter can be used offline.

What we do not collect

No personal contact profileWe do not collect email, phone, postal address, contacts, calendar, SMS, microphone recordings, precise GPS location, payment data, health data, or biometric data through the app.
No advertising useWe do not sell, rent, profile, or use cricket data for advertising or third-party marketing.

Permissions

  • Photos: optional, only when you choose a custom toss coin image.
  • Network: optional, for club sync, live follow, handover, and recovery.
  • Keep screen on: used while scoring or counting during a match.

Sharing, retention, and deletion

Cloud hosting is provided by Supabase as our infrastructure processor. Apple and Google may process standard install, crash, and store metadata under their own policies.

  • Local data stays until you reset it, delete matches, or uninstall UmpCall.
  • Club cloud data remains while the club exists. Admins can disband a club to delete club cloud data.
  • To request server-side deletion, email hello@gullydev.com with subject Privacy. We aim to respond within 30 days.

Security and rights

We use HTTPS/TLS for cloud features and backend access controls. Depending on where you live, you may have rights to access, correct, delete, export, or object to processing of personal data.

No ads. No analytics SDKs. Data not sold.

Contact

For privacy, deletion, support, or abuse reports, email hello@gullydev.com. Use subject Privacy for data requests and Report for offensive content.
