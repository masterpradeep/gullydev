QuiDit Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 11, 2026

Anonymous identity Friend rooms Optional notifications No ads

Privacy at a glance

QuiDit uses an anonymous app identity and stores only what the game needs: rooms, predictions, votes, display name, recovery key, and optional notification token. No ads. No data sale.

Terms Support

Information we collect

Account and identityQuiDit uses anonymous authentication and assigns a random user ID to your device. You may add a display name and recovery key.
Game dataPredictions, votes, rooms, leaderboard scores, results, and metadata needed to run the game.
NotificationsIf you enable push notifications, we store a push token for room and prediction alerts.

How we use information

  • Run rooms, predictions, votes, settlements, and leaderboards.
  • Restore your anonymous identity when you use a recovery key.
  • Send optional notifications and handle support or abuse reports.

We do not sell your personal information. We do not use your data for third-party advertising.

Where data is stored

QuiDit stores local app data on your device and cloud game data on backend infrastructure such as Supabase. Cloud traffic uses encrypted HTTPS/TLS connections.

Sharing

Your display name, predictions, votes, and results are visible to members of rooms you join. We share data only with infrastructure providers needed to run the service, notification services you enable, or when required by law or safety obligations.

Your choices

ProfileChange or remove your display name in the app profile.
NotificationsDisable push notifications in device settings.
RoomsLeave rooms you no longer want to use.
DeleteDelete your account and cloud data, or just local data, from the app profile. See Deleting your data.

Deleting your data

You can delete your QuiDit data at any time, directly in the app.

Delete account and cloud dataIn the app, open Profile, then tap Delete account and cloud data. This permanently removes your profile, rooms you own, your predictions and votes, and your recovery key from our servers. This cannot be undone.
Delete local data onlyIn the app, open Profile, then tap Delete local data. This clears rooms, predictions, votes, and scores stored on that device.

Lost access to your device? Email hello@gullydev.com with subject QuiDit Privacy and we will delete your server data within 30 days.

Predictions and votes in shared rooms may be anonymized rather than fully removed, to preserve room results for other members. Backups are purged on their normal cycle.

Retention and security

We keep data while your account is active and as needed to operate QuiDit. Account deletion removes associated cloud data within a reasonable period, subject to backups and legal obligations.

We use encrypted transport and backend access controls. QuiDit is not directed at children under 13 or the minimum age in your country.

Contact

Questions about privacy or deletion? Email hello@gullydev.com with subject QuiDit Privacy.
