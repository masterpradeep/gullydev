Privacy Support

The service

Our Innings provides cricket tools including digital toss, ball-by-ball scorebook, umpire counter, optional club cloud, match handover, player stats, and live match follow.

Our Innings is a scoring utility. It is not an official cricket board, ICC-certified scoring system, gambling product, or replacement for official league records.

Accounts and club access

No email loginCloud features use an anonymous session on your device.
Roles by codeAdmin, scorer, and viewer access are assigned by which join code is used.

Treat club join codes, live match codes, recovery codes, and handover pass codes like passwords. Your club is responsible for keeping them confidential.

Access codes

  • Scorer join code: trusted scorers can edit rosters and score for your club.
  • Viewer join code: read-only members can view club rosters and match history.
  • Club ID plus scorer code: owner recovery on a new device.
  • Live match code: spectators can follow that match's scoreboard.
  • Handover pass code: a substitute scorer can take over scoring mid-match.

User content and acceptable use

You are responsible for team names, player names, club names, match data, and custom images you enter. Do not enter content that is illegal, harassing, hateful, defamatory, sexually explicit, infringing, or impersonating others.

  • Do not bypass club role restrictions or access another club's data without authorization.
  • Do not brute-force join, recovery, live match, or handover codes.
  • Do not spam club creation, recovery attempts, or live publishes.
  • Do not use Our Innings for gambling or wagering on toss outcomes.
  • Do not attempt to disrupt, reverse-engineer, or abuse backend systems.

Reports and moderation
